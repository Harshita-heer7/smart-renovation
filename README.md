#  Smart Renovation ‚Äî Aesthetic Maintenance & Estimation App

Smart Renovation is a Kivy-based desktop/mobile app that simplifies **home renovation and maintenance**.  
It lets users estimate costs for painting, plumbing, tiling, and other services with a clean, dark aesthetic UI.

---

##  Features

**Smart Cost Estimation**
- Predicts material and labor costs for painting, plumbing, tiles, and renovation work.
- Uses ML models (if available) to generate accurate estimates.

 **File Uploads & Visual Quotes**
- Upload site photos (JPG/PNG) for better quote accuracy.
- Shows previews and full-size image views.

 **Service Providers**
- Verified local providers with ratings and contact details.
- Quick ‚ÄúCall‚Äù and ‚ÄúCopy Number‚Äù actions.

 **Site Visit Scheduling**
- Schedule on-site inspections for better planning.

 **AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract)**
- Subscribe to monthly or yearly maintenance plans.

 **History & Records**
- Saves all quotes and visits in SQLite.
- Exports to CSV for future ML retraining.

 **Aesthetic Dark UI**
- Teal-accent theme with gradient backgrounds.
- Smooth animations and organized layout.
